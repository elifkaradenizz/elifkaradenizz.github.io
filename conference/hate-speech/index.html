Conference Presentation

Hate Speech & Sexism Detection

In this project, natural language processing (NLP) techniques were used to detect and analyze sexism and hate speech on social media platforms. During the preprocessing stage, tokenization, lemmatization, and stop-word removal were applied using the NLTK library.

Text data was transformed into semantic representations using Word2Vec, and WordCloud visualization was used to highlight frequently used terms. Additionally, multiple machine learning algorithms were implemented to classify harmful content.

This work was presented at an international conference and later published as an academic paper.

Contributors: Elif Karadeniz, Fatmanur Polat, Beyzanur Çelik
